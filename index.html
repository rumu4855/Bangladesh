The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Developed nation

Becoming a developed country by 2041 with a per capita income of more than $12,500. Make Bangladesh a middle-income country

Poverty Eradication

Ending absolute poverty by 2031 and reducing poverty to less than 3% by 2041. Providing modern city amenities at the village level

Skilled human resources

Developing a skilled and creative workforce through ICT education and training. Increase life expectancy to 80 years.
